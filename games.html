Marvel Games es la marca editorial de videojuegos basada en las propiedades de Marvel, y es la división de videojuegos de Marvel Entertainment. Antes de la incorporación de Marvel Games, los videojuegos basados en propiedades de Marvel lanzados entre 1982 y 1985 fueron manejados por Marvel Comics Group, con los videojuegos de Marvel de 1986 a 1998 a cargo de Marvel Entertainment Group, mientras que los videojuegos basados en propiedades de Marvel antes de la incorporación de Marvel Games eran manejados directamente por Marvel Enterprises.

Spider-Man is a vertical scrolling action game written by Laura Nikolich for the Atari 2600, and released in 1982 by Parker Brothers.[1] It was both the first video game to feature Spider-Man and the first Marvel Comics based video game.

Questprobe featuring The Hulk is a 1984 graphic adventure video game developed and published by Adventure International in collaboration with Marvel Comics and Commodore Business Machines. It is the first installment in Questprobe, a series of graphic adventure games.

The player takes the role of Spider-Man, with powers including super strength and the ability to climb walls. Unlike previous releases, this game was not limited to simple verb/noun input, and could accept complex sentences
